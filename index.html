Why MoonCat

Reflections, reborn on a new chain.

SafeMoon made reflections famous: a token that pays you for holding it. MoonCat brings that mechanic to Robinhood Chain first — 1.5% of every trade is split across all holders, proportional to what you hold, the instant the trade settles.

No dashboards, no lockups, no gas to claim. Your wallet balance simply grows while the cat naps.
